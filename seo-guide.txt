# SEO Optimization Guide for Easy Cash for Car NZ

## Page Title Optimization
Each city page should have a unique, keyword-rich title like:
- `Cash for Car Auckland | Sell Your Car Fast in Auckland | Easy Cash for Car NZ`
- `Christchurch Car Buyers | Get Cash for Your Car in Christchurch | Easy Cash for Car NZ`

## Meta Description Template
Each page needs a unique meta description (150-160 characters):
`Get cash for your car in [City Name] with Easy Cash for Car NZ. Fast, reliable service. Free quote in minutes. Sell your car today!`

## Header Structure (H1, H2, H3)
```
H1: Cash for Car [City Name] - Fast & Reliable Service
H2: Why Choose Our Car Buying Service in [City Name]?
H2: How to Sell Your Car for Cash in [City Name]
H3: Steps to Sell Your Car
H3: Required Documents
H2: Areas We Serve Near [City Name]
```

## Internal Linking Strategy
- Link from city pages to the main service pages
- Cross-link between related city pages
- Use descriptive anchor text with keywords

## Local SEO Elements
- Include city name in URLs: `/auckland` or `auckland-car-buyers.html`
- Add local schema markup with city-specific details
- Include local landmarks and neighborhoods in content where relevant

## Image Optimization
- Use descriptive file names: `auckland-car-pickup.jpg`
- Include alt text: `alt="Cash for car service pickup in Auckland"`
- Compress images for faster loading

## Content Elements for Each City Page
1. Introduction paragraph about service in that city
2. List of neighborhoods served
3. Local testimonials (if available)
4. Common car models bought in that region
5. Average response time in that city
6. Local regulations or requirements
