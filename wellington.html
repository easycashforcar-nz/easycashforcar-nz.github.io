How do you determine the value of my car in Wellington?

Our Wellington valuations are based on multiple factors: (1) Make, model, and year, (2) Current odometer reading, (3) Overall condition (exterior, interior, mechanical), (4) WOF status and registration currency, (5) Market demand for that specific model in Wellington, (6) Recent sales data from Wellington market, (7) Parts value if vehicle is for scrap, (8) Current metal and recycling prices. We use real-time Wellington market data and consider local factors like salt air corrosion common in coastal areas, wear from hilly terrain, and demand for certain vehicle types popular in the region. Our goal is to offer fair market value that reflects what buyers are actually paying in Wellington right now.

Do I need a current WOF to sell my car to you?

No, you don't need a current WOF! We buy vehicles with expired WOF, no WOF at all, or those that won't pass WOF inspection. While a current WOF can increase the value we offer (typically by $200-$500), we still purchase vehicles without valid WOF certificates. If your car has failed WOF or you've decided it's not worth the repair costs to get it through inspection, we'll still make you a fair offer based on its current condition. This is especially common in Wellington where salt air and hilly terrain can cause wear that makes WOF compliance expensive.

Can you buy my car if it's financed or has money owing?

Yes, we can work with financed vehicles! If you have a car loan through a bank or finance company, we can help facilitate the sale. The process involves: (1) Getting a payout figure from your lender, (2) We make an offer on your vehicle, (3) If our offer exceeds the payout amount, we can pay your lender directly and give you the difference, (4) If there's negative equity (you owe more than the car's worth), you'll need to cover the difference. We've helped many Wellington residents in this situation and can guide you through the process. Contact us to discuss your specific circumstances.

We offer two instant payment methods: (1) Cash payment on the spot - we bring physical cash to your location when we collect the vehicle (popular option in Wellington), (2) Instant bank transfer - we can transfer funds directly to your account via internet banking while at your location, confirmed within minutes.
